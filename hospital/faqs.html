Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the Healthcare Portal?

The Healthcare Portal is a digital platform that allows users to find hospitals, manage queues, book appointments, and access their health records all in one place.

2. How do I create a Health ID?

You can create a Health ID by visiting the "Create Health ID" page, filling out your personal information, and submitting the form.

3. How can I find nearby hospitals?

By using the "Find Hospitals" feature, you can search for hospitals based on your location or specific requirements. The system will show a list of hospitals along with contact information.

4. How do I manage my queue at a hospital?

Once you book an appointment, the "Manage Queue" feature will allow you to see your current position in the queue, estimated waiting times, and notifications from the hospital.

5. Can I book appointments through the portal?

Yes, you can book appointments with doctors or specialists through the portal. After booking, you'll receive a confirmation and details on the appointment.

6. Is my personal information safe on the portal?

Yes, we use encryption and other security measures to ensure that your personal and medical data is kept secure. Your privacy is a top priority.

7. How do I contact support?

If you need assistance, you can reach out to our support team through the "Contact" page, where you'll find our email and phone number.
